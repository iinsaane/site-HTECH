Departnents Departments have been designed to better organize the team, so that each member and volunteer can focus on the field(s) in which they have strengths. Also, in addition to the individual work of each department, there is a permanent collaboration between all of them to maximize results in all areas. Mechanical department

The mechanical department deals with everything related to the construction, movement and forces that produce the robot's movement. They use the application of principles and techniques to solve engineering problems, from the design to the material manufacture of the robot, combining creativity, knowledge and analytical tools to complete the difficult task of turning an idea into reality. Programming department

The programming department handles the process of writing code in Android Studio, using Road Runner as well. Programmers create instructions for the computer to execute by writing and testing the code that allows the robot to function successfully both to the commands given by the drivers and to the automation part. (apoi o poză reprezentativă)

3D design department

The 3D design department deals with the process of using Onshape, Fusion and Blender to create objects in a three-dimensional space, so as to be able to make the parts necessary to make the robot, being in permanent contact with the mechanical department. They also deal with everything involved in using 3D printers and overseeing the printing process. (apoi o poză reprezentativă)

PR + Marketing department

The PR and Marketing department deals with the team's promotion, external communication and obtaining sponsorships. The department captures photos and videos and creates materials that they then edit to have content, while developing marketing strategies for social media promotion. It also deals with what it means to communicate with other teams and businesses in building and maintaining collaborations. (apoi o poză reprezentativă)

Design and editing department

The design and editorial department deals with what it means to build the technical book and the design of promotional materials. (apoi o poză reprezentativă)
